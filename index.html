The 10 Strategic Truths
1. Volume ≠ profit — top 20% of SKUs drive ~80% of gross profit
Why this matters ↓
Rationalizing the bottom 30–40% of SKUs could eliminate 3–6% revenue leakage and free working capital currently trapped in slow-moving inventory. Immediate action: commission SKU-level P&L analysis.
2. Geography matters more than product count — 3–5 markets drive ROIC
Why this matters ↓
Current 95% US concentration creates catastrophic single-point-of-failure risk. EU, UK, and Middle East markets offer immediate diversification — Tastee Choice brand is transferable with modest investment.
3. Data blindness is the biggest operational risk
Why this matters ↓
Fragmented ERP/data infrastructure means all decisions are reactive. Real-time demand sensing and pricing optimization are estimated to yield 1–2% margin improvement alone.
4. Distributors control margin — 30% drive 70% of sell-through
Why this matters ↓
Concentration in a small number of distributors creates leverage risk. Proactive partnership with top performers while diversifying the remainder is recommended within 90 days.
5. AI is leverage, not garnish — demand sensing and freight are real ROI
Why this matters ↓
Freight is a silent margin killer: partial containers, reactive booking, and poor load planning represent recoverable costs. AI-driven logistics optimization is the highest-ROI technology investment available today.
